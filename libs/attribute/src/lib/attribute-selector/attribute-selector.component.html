Attribute Selection

There are two types of attribute. Primary and Secondary. Primary attributes are increasing your passive stats while affecting bonus modifier for your secondary attributes. If you have more strength then you will have bonus ratio on stamina points. if you have 2 as bonus ratio it means each secondary attribute points will counted as double.

Strength: will increase base physical damage.

Agility: will increase base attackspeed/castingspeed.

Intelligence: will increase base cost reduction.


Secondary attributes are giving certain passive bonuses.

Stamina: will increase base health.

Speed: will increase base movement speed.

Willpower: will increase crowd control resistance.
